About Prince Arts

Your trusted partner in professional civil interior solutions

Our Story

Founded with a vision to transform spaces through exceptional craftsmanship by Mr. Rampratap Gautam, Prince Arts has been at the forefront of all the civil interior solutions for over 20 years.

What started as a small team of skilled craftsmen has grown into a trusted name in the Interior civil construction industry, known for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

We worked all over India with the likes of Inox cinemas, Reliance stores, Inorbit malls also other shopping complexes, Co-orperate buildings, Hospitals, Premium Hotels, Bungalows as well as Farmhouses and Residencial societies.

We believe that every ceiling tells a story, and we're here to help you create the perfect narrative for your space.
